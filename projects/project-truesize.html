Overview

Most people grow up with the Mercator projection burned into their mental model of the world. It's the map on classroom walls, in textbooks, and as the default in many apps — but it wildly exaggerates the size of countries near the poles. Greenland appears as large as Africa; in reality Africa is about 14× bigger.

The True True Size lets you search for any country or territory, place its outline on the map, and drag it freely across latitudes. As you move a shape, it redraws itself at the correct projected scale for that position, so you can make direct, honest comparisons. A live readout shows the Mercator scale factor, area inflation percentage, and your cursor's latitude — turning a frustrating cartographic lie into an intuitive learning tool.

Features
  • Search and place any country or territory outline on the Mercator map
  • Drag shapes freely across latitudes — geometry redraws in real time to reflect true projected scale
  • Stack multiple countries simultaneously for side-by-side size comparisons
  • Live sidebar shows cursor latitude, Mercator scale factor, and area inflation percentage
  • Clean, distraction-free UI with a single-colour world basemap that keeps focus on the shapes
