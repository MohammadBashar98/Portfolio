Overview

Uber Energy Drink is a premium energy beverage brand built around a bold identity — black and red branding, a dragon emblem, and a clear message of performance and strength. The website was designed to reflect that identity digitally, giving the brand a strong online presence that matches the energy of the product itself.

The project involved designing a product-first experience where visitors can explore the full lineup of flavors, understand the ingredients and nutritional profile, and find where to purchase. The visual direction was guided entirely by the brand's existing language: dark backgrounds, bold red accents, and confident typography that communicates power without clutter.

One of the key challenges was presenting multiple distinct product lines — from the flagship Dragon edition to the Ginkgo Guarana Premium and the Coconut & Açaí Edition — in a way that felt cohesive yet gave each flavor its own personality on screen.

Features
  • Full product showcase with individual pages for each flavor variant and edition
  • Ingredient transparency section — Taurine, 150mg Caffeine, Vitamins B2/B3/B5/B6/B12 displayed clearly
  • Bold hero section with brand positioning: "Pure Energy. Maximum Focus."
  • Mobile-responsive layout optimized for on-the-go browsing
  • Where-to-buy section linking to retail and online distribution channels
  • Brand story section communicating the meaning behind the dragon emblem and the name "Uber"
  • Social media integration connecting to the official Instagram and Facebook presence
